Simple rules. Serious competition.

Drop → Purchase → Scan → Puzzle → Submit

Here’s the exact flow GG will run every month.

1) Friday Drop (12:00 PM)

A featured location is revealed inside the platform.

2) Qualifying Purchase

Buy $20+ to receive a QR code on your receipt.

3) Scan to Unlock

Scan QR → your account logs it → puzzle pieces appear.

4) Assemble Pieces

Drag pieces into place; correct placement locks.

5) Final Friday

Scan the final QR and receive a crossword that forms a sentence.

6) Submit to Win

Submit the final sentence. 1st/2nd/3rd winners are awarded.

Fair play notes

Anti-cheat principle

QR codes must be unique + time-limited (server-verified later).

Community principle

The game should bless local businesses while players compete.
